What is Microtia?

Microtia is a congenital condition where a baby is born with an underdeveloped outer ear. It affects about 1 in 6,000 to 12,000 babies.

Children with Microtia are just like any other children‚Äîthey play, learn, dream, and deserve to see themselves as heroes in their own stories.

Little Ear, Big Heart was written to give children with Microtia a character they can identify with, and to help all children understand that differences make us special.

From a Father's Heart

Hi, I'm Jacob Rosales. I wrote Little Ear, Big Heart for my daughter, who was born with Microtia.

When she was born, I searched for books that could help her understand her condition‚Äîbooks where she could see herself as the hero. When I couldn't find one, I decided to write it.
